Apple Smoothie

Apple Smoothie that tastes like liquid fall.

Ingredients

  • 1 large Fuji apple, cored and sliced
  • 3/4 cup whole milk plain Greek yogurt
  • 1/4 cup unsweetened apple juice
  • 3 tablespoons rolled oats
  • 1 tablespoon almond butter
  • 2 teaspoons maple syrup, or to taste
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 pinch ground cloves

Steps:

  1. Place banana, apple, yogurt, apple juice, oats, almond butter, maple syrup, cinnamon, nutmeg, and cloves into the cup of a high speed blender
  2. Blend until smooth
  3. If desired, add ice for a thicker smoothie, or add milk for a thinner consistency.
